OUR TECHNIQUE

Advanced Smelting Techniques for Superior Results

Lualaba Copper Smelter employs state-of-the-art smelting techniques and technologies to ensure optimal recovery of valuable metals while minimizing environmental impact. Our advanced methods include flash smelting, continuous casting, and energy-efficient furnaces, allowing us to achieve superior product quality and operational efficiency. We continuously invest in research and development to stay at the forefront of smelting innovation.
